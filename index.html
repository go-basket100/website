About GoBasket

GoBasket was founded to solve a simple challenge: helping customers access their favourite stores and restaurants quickly and conveniently.

We provide reliable last-mile delivery solutions for restaurants, supermarkets, retail stores, pharmacies and individual customers throughout Rustenburg.

Our Mission

To provide fast, reliable, and affordable delivery services that connect customers with the products and services they need.

Our Vision

To become Rustenburg's most trusted and preferred local delivery partner.
